COVID-19 Convalescent Plasma Donation

The Delhi government has launched the countryâ€™s first plasma bank. If you are a recovered Corona patient,
come forward and donate your plasma. Your contribution may help save a life!

You can donate plasma if:
1. You were tested positive for COVID-19
2. You have fully recovered and are free of symptoms for 14 days
3. You are between 18-60 years old
You cannot donate plasma if:
1. Your weight is less than 50 kg
2. You have ever been pregnant
3. You are diabetic on insulin
4. Your Blood Pressure is more than 140 and diastolic less than 60
or more than 90
5. You have uncontrolled diabetes or hypertension with change in medication in
last 28 days
6. You are a Cancer Survivor
7. You have chronic kidney/heart/lung or liver disease.
